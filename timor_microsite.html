Dili - National Context

Understanding the national policy framework and governance structures that enable social accountability

National Policy Environment

Timor-Leste's commitment to decentralized governance and community participation creates the enabling environment for social accountability initiatives. National health policies emphasize community engagement and local ownership of health service improvements.

Ministry of Health

National leadership and policy direction for community-based health initiatives

Decentralization Framework

Legal and institutional structures supporting local governance and accountability

Civil Society Partnership

Ermera Municipality - Supporting Mechanisms

The crucial intermediary level connecting national policy with community implementation

Municipal Coordination Role

Ermera Municipality serves as the vital link between national health policy and community-level implementation, providing coordination, resources, and technical support to enable effective social accountability mechanisms.

Administrative Support

Municipal health office coordinates resources and provides administrative backing for community initiatives

Capacity Building

Training and technical assistance for community health workers and local leaders

Inter-Suku Coordination

Facilitating knowledge sharing and best practice exchange between different Sukus
